Dragon's Den

The mascot for every risky idea that might still be worth it.

This room is intentionally half joke and half mission statement. Research gets better when there is at least one space reserved for bold, failure-prone bets.

What the dragon stands for

  • Taking on ideas that do not fit cleanly into standard playbooks.
  • Keeping experimentation playful enough that curiosity survives the grind.
  • Remembering that the hard thing is often the interesting thing.

Yes, the dragon is still attackable on the map if you walk close enough and hit ACTION.
